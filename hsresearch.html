High School Research Project

Overview

I started working on the high school research project in my sophomore year and continued for three years. In this project, I worked with Dr. Jason Keleher, Professor James Hofmann, and graduate students from Lewis University. This project was studying to find a way to prevent laser strikes on airplanes by using liquid crystal technologies.

Collaboration

This project was a partnership between Downers Grove High School South Research Club and Lewis University's Department of Chemistry. In this collaboration, Downers Grove South High School's Robitics Club assisted in 3D printing certain materials.
